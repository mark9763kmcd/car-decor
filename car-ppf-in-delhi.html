QUESTION AND ANSWERS

Q. Do new cars require PPF?

A. If you have a new car and don't want to see scratches, swirls, or other types of damage on your paint, it's a good idea to think about getting PPF. This will help your car look brand new for a longer time and increase its resale value.

Q. What is the best thickness for PPF in a car?

A. The basic need is to have a transparent layer, glue, and protective sheet that are 140 to 200 microns thick. Curtain brands might also provide the features of self-repairing abilities.

Q. What is the minimum cost of paint protection film for cars?

A. The cost depends on the PPF brand and type of your vehicle. Generally, the cost starts at Rs. 45,000 and goes up to Rs. 1,50,000+ in Delhi, NCR. Avail a 12-month no-cost EMI option on credit cards. Check out top-branded paint protection films at the best price.

Q. What is TPU in PPF?

A. Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is an important material in paint protection film (PPF). It helps make PPF that provides excellent protection without changing color for a long time.

Q. What is the better choice between TPU and TPH for car PPF?

A. TPU is widely regarded as the best option for paint protection film, as it provides greater flexibility and durability compared to TPH. TPU films are extremely flexible, can be easily installed without heat guns, and are resistant to cracking or color fading.
