SERVICES OF Door2Door Car Wash

Door2door Car Wash is a trusted brand in the car cleaning, detailing, sanitising, and dry-cleaning fields, as well as sofa dry cleaning and home goods cleaning. Through our service partners on all sites, we are expanding our services to every car owner.

Foam Wash

Snow foam is intended to soften the dust and grime on the exterior of your vehicle. After a short length of time, it naturally begins to drip down the surface of your car, picking up a lot of dirt in the process.

Its foam can handle more particles than a conventional car washing can, making the surface of the car less sensitive to harm during the cleaning process. It is more penetrative and ensures that dirt loosens quickly and easily.

Interior Deep Dry Clean

Dry cleaning is the process of cleaning your automobile with a cleaning product that is a dry solvent, meaning it does not include any substantial moisture.

Professional specialists are aware of the fabric of expensive interiors that may be harmed using inferior local cleaning chemicals; however, professionals always use a good quality branded cleaning agent with the proper dilution can save the fabric and extend its lifespan.

Every automobile owner must guarantee adequate cleaning and dusting before embarking on a journey, or at the absolute least schedule for a vacuum cleaning.

Sofa Cleaning

We are Professional sofa cleaners, can eliminate 99.1% of typical household allergens from upholstery, revitalising your home. We eliminate those unsanitary materials, keeping your furnishings and air fresher for longer.

Rubbing Polish

Rubbing is a method that removes stains and blemishes from the paint surface of your automobile and gives it a smooth and clean appearance. It removes the unclean exterior coat of paint from your automobile, removing scratches and other flaws.

It is suggested for all types of automobile paint finishes to remove scratches and oxidation layer. The rubbing substance smoothen and peels the surface, making it ready for polishing.

Teflon Coating

Teflon coating is also known as a paint protection coating or an anti-scratch coating. Teflon is technically a fluoropolymer that was manufactured chemically. Chemours, a DuPont company, discovered the Teflon chemical compound first.

Teflon coating services are provided both at the customer's premises and at our Car Detailing Studio outlets.
